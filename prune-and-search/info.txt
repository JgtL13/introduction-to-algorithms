
A program implementing the prune and search algorithm on a selection problem.
The program will generate 100 random numbers in range 1 to 1000.
The user will then input a number K from keyboard.
The program will then find the Kth largest number in the random numbers generated.
Output consists number of numbers deleted and p used for every round.

Sample input:
32

Sample output(value will vary):
p : 370
Amount of numbers deleted : 42
p : 681
Amount of numbers deleted : 29
p : 572
Amount of numbers deleted : 15
p : 623
Amount of numbers deleted : 6
p : 659
Amount of numbers deleted : 5
p : 669
Amount of numbers deleted : 2
p : 663
32th largest number found : 663
